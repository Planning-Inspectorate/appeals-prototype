Commenting on an appeal is optional.

Request a visit to your property

If the inspector cannot see the appeal site from the road, you can request a visit to your property. If they agree, they’ll visit your property with Rachel Silver and the planning officer from Bristol City Council. They’ll do this on the same day as the appeal site visit.

You can request a visit to your property by emailing enquiries@planninginspectorate.gov.uk

What happens next

The inspector will review all the evidence and make their decision on the appeal. The Planning Inspectorate will send you an email explaining the final decision.
