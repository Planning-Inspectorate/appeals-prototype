Find an appeal

Use this service to find, view or comment on a planning appeal. Commenting on a planning appeal is also called ‘making a representation’.

You can find an appeal using either the:

  • site postcode
  • 7 digit appeal reference number

An appeal will either be:

  • open for comment
  • closed for comment
  • complete and have an appeal decision

You can only comment on an ‘open’ appeal.

Comment on an appeal

You’ll need to give your full name and an email address, so that we can contact you about your comment. We will not share your contact details.

Do not include any comments that you already submitted to a planning application. The Planning Inspectorate will consider all application comments when they review the appeal.

You must not include sensitive information. If your comment has sensitive information, we will send it back to you and will not include it in the appeal.
