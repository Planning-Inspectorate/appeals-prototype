An appeal has been made to the Secretary of State against the refusal of planning permission. The Planning Inspectorate will make the final decision on this appeal.

What you can do

If you have an interest in this appeal, you can have your say and add a comment (this is also known as a ‘written representation’).

The deadline to comment is 11:59pm on {{ 7 | daysInFuture }}.

How to comment on the appeal

You can use the Planning Inspectorate’s website to submit a comment on this appeal. If you commented on the original application, it has already been sent to the planning inspector.

  1. Go to https://comment-on-planning-appeals.service.gov.uk/
  2. Search for appeal reference {{ appealCode }}.
  3. View more details and add your comment.

Alternatively, you can send your comment by post to: Appeals Team, The Planning Inspectorate, Temple Quay House, 2 The Square, Bristol BS1 6PN.

What happens next

The Planning Inspectorate will review all the information from the application, comments on the appeal and may do a site visit. They will then make their decision. We will publish the decision on our website.
