How to claim

You make a claim by submitting a document to the Planning Inspectorate.

The document must explain the unreasonable behaviour and how it cost you money. You do not need to include a detailed breakdown of the costs or receipts.

For some types of appeal, you must submit your claim with your appeal. For other types, you can submit it by emailing your case officer during your appeal.

Find out more about your deadline to claim for costs.
