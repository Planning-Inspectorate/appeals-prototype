Before you continue

You cannot save your progress and come back later. Before you continue, make sure you have everything you need for your appeal.

Check what you need to submit

You need to submit these documents with your appeal:

  • your planning application form
  • the plans, drawings and supporting documents you submitted with your application
    • {% if data["appealsub-"+version+"-bys-permissiongrantedrefused"] != "I have not received a decision" %}
  • the decision letter from your local planning department
    • {% endif %}
  • your design and access statement, if you submitted one with your application
  • your appeal statement

If you do not have a copy of your planning application form, you can search for it on your local planning department's website.

You also have the option to submit:

  • plans or drawings that have been updated or created since your application
  • any other documents that you'd like to submit in support of your appeal
