The stages of an appeal

You can appeal a householder planning decision online.

You need to send a copy of your appeal, including all the supporting documents, to your local planning department. The Planning Inspectorate will tell you how to do this.

The appeal process

Check how long householder planning appeal decisions may take.

How your appeal will be decided​

Householder planning appeals are always decided by written representations, which means the Inspector looks at written evidence from all parties to reach a decision.​
