Appeal submitted

Your appeal has been submitted to the Planning Inspectorate.

You'll need to send a copy of your appeal to your local planning department.

What happens next?

  1. We will check we have everything we need to continue with your appeal. We'll contact you if we need more information.
  2. Your local planning department will send you a copy of their questionnaire and supporting documents for your information only, you do not need to do anything.
  3. An Inspector will need to make a site visit. We'll be in touch if the Inspector needs you to provide access.
  4. The Inspector will then consider all the evidence to make their decision.

We aim to make a decision on your appeal in around 8 weeks.
