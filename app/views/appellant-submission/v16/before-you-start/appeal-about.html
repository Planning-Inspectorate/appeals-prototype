What is a major development?

A housing development is major if it has either:

  • 10 or more houses
  • a site area of 0.5 hectares or more, and an unknown number of houses

A travelling and caravan pitch development is major if it has 10 or more pitches.

Any other type of development is major if it either:

  • creates 1000 square metres of floor space or more
  • has a site area of 1 hectare or more
