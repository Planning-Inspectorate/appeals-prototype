Did you apply for 'householder planning permission'?

'Householder planning permission' is a specific type of planning permission. You can check what you applied for on your original planning application form.

What is householder planning permission?

'Householder planning permission' covers projects for altering or enlarging a single house. These include:​

  • conservatories​
  • dormer windows​
  • extensions​
  • garages, car ports and outbuildings​
  • loft conversions
