What you need to submit your appeal

You will need to upload your:

  • planning application form
  • evidence of your agreement to change the description of development, if you need one
  • application decision letter from your local authority, if you have one
  • planning obligation, if you have one
  • separate ownership certificate and agricultural land declaration, if you need one
  • draft statement of common ground, if you need one
  • design and access statement, if you have one
  • plans, drawings and supporting documents for your application
  • appeal statement, if you have one
  • new plans or drawings to support your appeal
  • other documents to support your appeal
