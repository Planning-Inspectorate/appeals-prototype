Submit an appeal

Before you appeal, make sure you have:

  • your appeal statement
  • your planning application form
  • the site plan for your appeal site
  • the plans, drawings and supporting documents for your application
  • your design and access statement, if you have one
  • the decision letter from the local planning department, if you have one
  • your draft statement of common ground, if you need one

Find out more about the documents you need to prepare.

You should have already:

  • chosen how you think the Planning Inspectorate should decide your appeal
  • told any other landowners about your appeal

Find out more about what you should do before you appeal.
