To appeal the enforcement notice, you must have an interest in the land or be a relevant occupier of the land.

It does not matter what your status was at the time the enforcement notice was served. You must tell us your status at the time of making this appeal.

I'm a lender in the mortgage for the land or building.
I occupy the land or building rented from a landlord.
I occupy the land or building with the owner’s consent.
