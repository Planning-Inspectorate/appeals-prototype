What you can do

You can respond to the statements in writing. This is called your ‘final comments’.

Your comments must only respond to the issues in the statements. You do not need to repeat information from your appeal statement. You cannot introduce new evidence or arguments. Do not include any personal or sensitive information.

  1. Go to the link to receive a code: www.gov.uk/appeal-a-planning-decision/final-comments?app=d81vW7td12s4t5gff728y29
  2. Enter the code we send in an email to you.
