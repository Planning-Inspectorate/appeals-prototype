You can use this service to comment on an appeal.

Your comments

Any previous comments that you submitted as part of an application will be considered by the Inspector. The local planning department shares these with the Planning Inspectorate, so there is no need to submit repeat comments.

We will share any comments you submit with the applicant and the local planning department.

You should only include personal information if it's relevant to the appeal.

Personal information includes health, race and gender.

See a full list of what counts as personal information

Other examples of personal information

  • comments from children
  • information relating to children
  • information relating to health
  • information relating to crime

It also includes any information relating to an individual's:

  • race
  • ethnic origin
  • politics
  • religion
  • trade union membership
  • genetics
  • physical characteristics
  • sex life
  • sexual orientation

If you include any personal information that's not relevant, we'll ask you to resubmit. This could mean that you miss the deadline to submit comments.

Your comments must not include inflammatory or abusive language.

Before you start

You will need:

  • the 7-digit appeal reference that can be found on your letter from the local planning department.
  • an email address so we can contact you if we have any questions
  • your comments saved in a document
